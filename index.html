01Information We Access

Calculator Pro accesses only the following minimal, on-device information needed to operate. Nothing is ever sent externally.

Data TypePurposeStored Externally?
Calculation inputs & resultsPerform math, unit conversions, and finance resultsNever
Calculation historyAllow you to review past entries within the AppNever — local only
User preferences (theme, settings)Remember your chosen setup (e.g., Dark Mode)Never — local only
✅ Technical Guarantee The App does not request the android.permission.INTERNET permission. Without this permission, it is technically impossible for the App to transmit any data from your device to an external server.

02Information We Do NOT Collect

Calculator Pro does not access, collect, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Photos, media, or files from your storage
  • Contacts, call logs, or messages
  • Browsing history or device usage habits
  • Financial details or credit card numbers
  • Health or biometric data
  • Audio recordings or camera feedback
  • Unique device IDs (IMEI, advertising ID)
ℹ️ Zero-Server Architecture The App operates strictly on your localized hardware. We do not operate any backend servers or cloud databases for this App.

03How We Use Information

The only data the App touches is what you interact with on your screen. Here is exactly how it is used — entirely on your device:

  • Calculations: Math expressions are processed in-memory to produce results.
  • Calculation History: Entries are saved in the App's private local database for your convenience. This data can be cleared at any time.
  • Preferences: Settings like "Shake to Clear" or "Formula History" are saved locally in Android's SharedPreferences.
  • Tactile Feedback: If enabled, the App triggers your device's vibration motor for button-press feedback. No data is logged.

04Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

  • RAM during session: Transient data used during calculation is discarded when the App is closed.
  • Private local database: History is stored in a private sandboxed SQLite database, inaccessible to other apps on your device.
  • SharedPreferences: Your configuration is stored in the App's private configuration space.

Security Measures

  • Android Sandbox: The App is isolated from other software via standard Android sandboxing.
  • No network transmission: The physical lack of internet permission prevents any data leak.
  • No Analytics SDKs: We do not include any tracking libraries (like Firebase or Facebook SDK).

05Permissions Explained

Calculator Pro requests only one permission, solely for UI feedback:

PermissionPurposeImpact if Denied
VIBRATE Tactile feedback when pressing buttons. No data is read. The App works normally; vibration will be disabled.

06Third-Party Services

To provide core Android functionality, we integrate minimal Google Play Core services which operate strictly for system-level features:

  • Google Play In-App Updates: Checks the Play Store for the latest version of the app to ensure security and stability.
  • Google Play In-App Review: Allows you to rate the app directly from within the UI.
⚠️ Service Disclosure While the App itself is offline and collects no data, these system-level Google Play interactions are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. These services only activate when you manually "Check for Updates" or choose to rate the App.

07Data Sharing & Disclosure

Calculator Pro does not share, sell, or disclose any user data to third parties. We technically cannot do so, as we do not collect or possess any user data on our own servers.

08Your Rights & Controls

  • Clear History: Wipe all local calculation logs via the App settings at any time.
  • Full Data Removal: Clearing the App's "Storage & Cache" in Android System Settings or uninstalling the App will permanently delete all local history and preferences.

09Children's Privacy
