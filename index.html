01Information We Access

Calculator Pro accesses only the following minimal, on-device information needed to operate. Nothing is ever sent externally.

Data TypePurposeStored Externally?

✅ Technical Guarantee The App does not request the android.permission.INTERNET permission. Without this permission, it is technically impossible for the App to send any data off your device.

Calculator Pro does not access, collect, or process any of the following:

  • Location data (GPS, network-based, or IP-based)
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Contacts or call logs
  • SMS, MMS, or messaging content

  • Health or biometric data

ℹ️ Zero-Server Architecture We do not operate any servers, cloud infrastructure, or databases for this App. There is no backend to send data to.

03How We Use Information

The only data the App touches is what you type into the calculator. Here is exactly how it is used — entirely on your device:

  • Calculations: Numbers and expressions you enter are processed in real time on your device to produce results. Nothing is logged or recorded remotely.
  • Calculation History: Previous calculations are saved in the App's private local database so you can scroll back through past results. This history never leaves your device and can be cleared by you at any time.
  • Preferences: Settings such as your chosen theme, sound, or vibration preferences are saved locally in Android's SharedPreferences so the App remembers your setup between sessions.
  • Tactile Feedback: If enabled, the App triggers your device's vibration motor when a button is pressed. No data is recorded from this interaction.
ℹ️ No Secondary Use We do not aggregate, analyze, or profile your usage for any purpose beyond making the calculator work on your screen.

04Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

  • RAM only during a session: Calculations in progress are held in device memory and discarded when you close the App.
  • Private local database: Calculation history is stored in a private SQLite database inside the App's sandboxed storage area on your device — inaccessible to other apps.
  • SharedPreferences: Your app settings are stored in the App's private sandboxed preferences, not accessible outside the App.
  • No external storage: We write nothing to your SD card, shared storage, cloud services, or remote servers.

Security Measures

  • Android Sandbox: The App runs in Android's application sandbox, isolating it from other apps and system components.
  • No network transmission: Without the INTERNET permission, no data can physically leave your device.
  • No third-party SDKs: No external libraries are included that could access or transmit data.
🔐 Data Retention Your calculation history is retained locally on your device until you choose to clear it. We hold no copies of it anywhere. Uninstalling the App deletes all local data permanently.

Permissions We Deliberately Do NOT Request

⚠️ Privacy Protections by Design The following permissions are intentionally absent from the App's manifest:
  • INTERNET — Cannot send any data to external servers
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — Cannot check network status
  • READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — Cannot access your files or photos
  • CAMERA / RECORD_AUDIO — Cannot capture images or sound
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — Cannot track location
  • READ_CONTACTS / READ_CALL_LOG / READ_SMS — Cannot access personal communications
  • READ_PHONE_STATE — Cannot read device identifiers

08Your Rights & Controls

Calculation History

  • View history: Scroll through past calculations within the App's history panel.
  • Clear history: Delete your full calculation history at any time from within the App's settings or history screen.

Permission Management

  • Vibration: Go to Android Settings → Apps → Calculator Pro → Permissions to enable or disable vibration at any time. The App functions fully without it.

Full Data Removal

  • Clear Data: Android Settings → Apps → Calculator Pro → Storage → Clear Data — removes all history and preferences from your device instantly.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all associated local data. Nothing remains on any server, because none exists.
